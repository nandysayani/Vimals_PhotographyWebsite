Vimal Chandran(b. 1984) is a visual artist and illustrator who works in a variety of media, he plays with words and brushes to actualize his ideas on paper. A transcendent artist, he sways across boundaries of time and space and create images that take us to a psychedelic experience of colors and imagination. By applying a poetic and often metaphorical language, his works appear as dreamlike images in which fiction and reality meet, well-known tropes merge, meanings shift, past and present fuse. Ardent fan of pop and mass culture, he considers art as an instrument to propagate ideas. His works demonstrate how life extends beyond its own subjective limits and often tell stories about the effects of global cultural interaction over the latter half of the twentieth century. He challenges the binaries we continually reconstruct between Self and Other, between our own ‘cannibal’ and ‘civilized’ selves. A man of strong political and ideological identities, he urges us to look at the social and political injustices happening in and around us through the lens of art. Hailing from Palakkad, Kerala, Vimal Chandran has always been inclined towards arts and culture. He did his B.Tech in Information Technology from University of Calicut, Kerala. Although Vimal kept his analytic mind active with his technical expertise, he always explored the art world to the finest detail that led the creative side of his mind to bloom. After working with top MNCs in IT industry as a software engineer for seven years ,he decided to move from a part time freelancer to full time Visual artist. Vimal Chandran currently lives and works in Bangalore,Karnataka as a freelance illustrator,photographer and a visual artist .
